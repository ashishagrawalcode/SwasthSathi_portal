Government Health Schemes

Information on key government programs to support rural health and wellness.

Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

Free health insurance up to ₹5 lakh per family, especially benefiting rural poor households.

Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)
